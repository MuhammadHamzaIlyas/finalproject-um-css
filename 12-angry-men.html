12 Angry Men

12 Angry Men

  • Director: Sidney Lumet
  • Genre: Crime, Drama
  • Year of Release: 1957

Sidney Lumet's courtroom drama unfolds almost entirely in a jury room, where 12 men debate the guilt or innocence of a young defendant accused of murder. The film is a gripping exploration of prejudice, justice, and the power of reasonable doubt, highlighting the dynamics of group decision-making and personal biases.
