Interstellar

Interstellar

  • Director: Christopher Nolan
  • Genre: Adventure, Drama, Sci-Fi
  • Year of Release: 2014

Directed by Christopher Nolan, Interstellar is a visually stunning science fiction epic about a group of astronauts traveling through a wormhole to find a new habitable planet for humanity. The film explores themes of love, sacrifice, and the survival of the human race, with a strong emphasis on the emotional bond between a father and his daughter.
