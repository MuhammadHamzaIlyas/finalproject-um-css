Schindler's List

Schindler's List

  • Director: Steven Spielberg
  • Genre: Biography, Drama, History
  • Year of Release: 1993

Steven Spielberg's harrowing historical drama chronicles the true story of Oskar Schindler, a German industrialist who saved over 1,000 Jews during the Holocaust by employing them in his factories. The film explores themes of human cruelty, redemption, and the resilience of the human spirit, with stark, haunting visuals in black and white.
