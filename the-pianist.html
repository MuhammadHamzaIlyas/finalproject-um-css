The Pianist

The Pianist

  • Director: Roman Polanski
  • Genre: Biography, Drama, Music
  • Year of Release: 2002

Roman Polanski's The Pianist tells the true story of Władysław Szpilman, a Jewish pianist in Warsaw during World War II, as he struggles to survive the Holocaust. The film is a poignant and deeply personal portrayal of survival, loss, and the enduring power of music amidst unimaginable suffering.
